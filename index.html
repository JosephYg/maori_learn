Text file format rules

Create a .txt file following these rules, then paste or upload it below.

# Words
kia ora | hello, thank you
whānau | family | extended family note
kai | food

# Sentences
Kei te pai ahau. | I am well.
Kia ora, e hoa. | Hello, friend.
  • # Words or # Sentences marks a section. Sections can appear multiple times.
  • Each entry on its own line, fields separated by |
  • Words: Māori | English or Māori | English | Note
  • Sentences: Māori | English
  • Lines starting with # or blank lines are ignored
  • Duplicates (same Māori text already in db) are skipped
